Welcome to DerpFest!

We are an AOSP based custom ROM, twisted with the latest features from all over the Android development community. Quality and stability is our first priority as we are dedicated to bringing you the best Android has to offer.

About Us


Introduction

We started as a small project based on AOSiP for the OnePlus 5T back on Android 9.0. Then various users and maintainers got interested in supporting this project. Now we made efforts to cut the cord and stand on our own feet. We try to be a platform for beginners and support the Custom ROM community with our own unique contributions as well. This ROM exists thanks to our inspiration - AOSiP, Thank you!

Why Do People Love DerpFest?

There are some features limited to Pixel devices like At a Glance Weather or Game Dashboard, which are now available to other AOSP devices. Some of the best additions by DerpFest are features like AOD icons on lockscreen, additions to Pulse like gravity options or round lines and optional beautiful handcrafted system icons from Niklas Schnettler.

Our success wouldn't have been possible without the support of our maintainers, testers and users who enjoy using and testing this ROM.
